Grand Opening

Bill and Paula White just opened up a new Bed and Breakfast in the Alpharetta area called the Golden Eagle Bed and Breakfast.

Amenities

The Golden Eagle Bed and Breakfast is a quiet and elegant establishment. All rooms come with a small refrigerator, microwave, and a 32 in. flat screen TV. There is a complimentary continental breakfast (Danish, bagels, orange juice, cereal, oatmeal, coffee and tea, etc.) Small pets are welcome for an additional fee. Please call to inquire about it. We also have a free shuttle bus that runs back and forth to the Avalon and Northpoint Mall areas upon request.

Banquet and Meeting Space

We have a hall that we rent out to prospective clients for parties, wedding receptions, etc. for a fee of $500. Golden Eagle guests will receive a 20% discount. Plenty of parking is available in the back for outside guests. To book the hall you must give at least a 60 day notice. There is a $100 non-refundable deposit required to book the space.

Live Music

Every thursday we have a jazz hour in the lobby to promote local artists, and all Golden Eagle guests are welcome to attend. Complimentary wine, cheese, and fruit platters are served at all of the events. Outside guests can attend for a $5.00 fee.
