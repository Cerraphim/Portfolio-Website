The White Family

Bill and Paula White have been married for over 20 years. They are both retired military who have traveled all over the world fighting for our country. They decided to open the Golden Eagle Bed and Breakfast to meet new people. They feel like surrounding themselves with different people daily helps to keep them young and live out their glory days happy and peaceful.
